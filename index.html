Frequently Asked Questions

Why cant I access the shared link after clicking?

You need to make sure your login to Dropbox via the browser. Otherwise is shows you the default view, which you can comment on the create.

Does it use up alot of space on my computer?

Not really. You just need to make sure your folders/files are 'Make Online-Only' after you finsihed using them.

Can I share files with others?

Yes, you can easily share files or folders with others by generating a share link or inviting them directly.

Do people see my files?

The folder with your name on it, is your personal folder. No one can access or see, unless you want to share files with them.

What happens if I delete a file from Dropbox?

Deleted files can be recovered from the "Deleted files" section within 30 days (or longer for certain plans). You can restore the file if needed, unless it’s been permanently deleted.

How do I recover old versions of files?

Dropbox allows you to recover previous versions of files for up to 30 days (or longer depending on your plan) via the version history feature. You can access this through the file’s options on the website or app.
